## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Is my money locked?**  
A: No. You can withdraw anytime (limited to 1,000 POL/day). Lock-up is optional and increases returns.

**Q: What if platform fails?**  
A: Funds are in smart contracts, not company wallets. Your POL stays yours even if platform shuts down.

**Q: Can I lose money?**  
A: No impermanent loss like LPs. Deposited amount is always yours. You can only gain from rewards.

**Q: What if rewards are unsustainable?**  
A: Treasury fallback ensures rewards even if staking fees dry up. Multiple safety nets in place.

**Q: Can I withdraw my lock-up early?**  
A: Yes, but you lose the lock-up bonus. Better to wait or accept the penalty if urgent.

**Q: Are there taxes?**  
A: Depends on your jurisdiction. Nuxchain doesn't withhold taxes—that's user responsibility.

**Q: Why Polygon instead of Ethereum?**  
A: 100x lower fees ($0.01 vs $10+ per transaction), sub-second settlement, same security as ETH.
